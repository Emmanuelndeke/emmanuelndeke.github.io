Vegetation Health Classification

This map tells an unexpected story. While Lake Chad is known for its long-term decline, my NDVI analysis reveals a different trend between 2017 and 2024. The 2024 data shows noticeable greening and a more consolidated water body compared to 2017. This doesn't solve the long-term crisis for the millions in Chad, Nigeria, Niger, and Cameroon who depend on it, but it highlights the lake's resilience and complexity.

Methodology

Supervised classification was performed using satellite imagery. Training samples were created for different land cover classes, and spatial analysis tools were used to generate the final classified map.

Tools Used

  • QGIS Software
  • Landsat Band 4&3 and Band 5&4 collections from USGS Earth Explorer
