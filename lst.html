Nairobi Land Satellite Temperature Classification

Spatial analysis of Land Satellite Temperature distribution using satellite imagery

Land Cover Distribution

The analysis identifies major land cover categories across Kenya including vegetation, built-up areas, and water bodies. Vegetation dominates western regions while arid landscapes cover much of northern Kenya.

Methodology

Satellite imagery was processed and classified to identify vegetation, water bodies, and built-up areas.

Tools Used

ArcGIS Pro and remote sensing spatial analysis.
